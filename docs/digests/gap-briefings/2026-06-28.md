- Model Card v2.0, Apr 13 2026. One face-on/DTL RGB video (≥120 FPS) → 3D skeleton + biomech metrics (torso/pelvis rotation & bend, sway/slide/thrust/lift, X-Factor, swing tempo, kinematic sequence P1/P3/P4/P5/P7/P8). Validated vs OptiTrack.
